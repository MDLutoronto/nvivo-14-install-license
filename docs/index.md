# Installing and Licensing NVivo 14

This tutorial describes how to request a license for NVivo 14, download it, and license it.

Table of Contents
=================

1\. [Requesting a License](#request)

2\. [Downloading, Installation, and Authorization (Windows)](#windows)

3\. [Downloading, Installation, and Authorization (Mac)](#mac)

Requesting a License
====================

1\. Check NVivo’s system requirements before downloading, using this [**link**](https://help-nv.qsrinternational.com/20/win/Content/about-nvivo/installation.htm).

2\. If you are currently faculty, staff, or a student at the University of Toronto, fill out the [**license request form**](https://mdl.library.utoronto.ca/technology/nvivo-software).

3\. If you don't already have a myNVivo account, you will need to create one first in order to download the software. Go to the [**myNVivo Portal**](https://portal.mynvivo.com).

4\. Once you're in the myNVivo Portal, either sign in if you already have a registered account, or click on “Sign up now.”  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/NewNew4.jpg' | relative_url }}' alt='myNVivo Portal with "Sign up now" link highlighted' title='' width='590' height='622' />  
 

5\. Fill in your details. Click “Send Verification Code”, and enter the code that is automatically sent to the inputted email address.  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/New6.jpg' | relative_url }}' alt='Create account panel with 'Send Verification Code' button highlighted' title='' width='590' height='873' />  
 

6\. Once the code has been entered and verified, click "Create Account".  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/New7.jpg' | relative_url }}' alt='Create account panel with 'Create Account' button highlighted' title='' width='605' height='851' />  
 

7\. Fill in the remaining details. Click "Complete Sign Up."  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/New8.jpg' | relative_url }}' alt=''Complete your Nivo Setup' panel with 'Complete Sign Up' button highlighted' title='' width='594' height='698' />  
 

 

Downloading, Installation, and Authorization (Windows)
======================================================

*Note: please be sure to download NVivo 14 specifically. The license key you received in your email will only work with NVivo 14\.*

1\. Log into [**myNVivo Portal**](https://portal.mynvivo.com/)using the account you just created. After logging in, under **Your Products** select **NVivo \> Launch.** 
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/NVivo_Download_1.png' | relative_url }}' alt='MyNVivo Page with Launch highlighted.' title='' width='1764' height='1080' />  
 

2\. In the NVivo window, select **Download NVivo for Windows.** Install NVivo 14 for Windows following the usual procedure for installations on your computer. Follow the instructions below OR watch this [installation and authorization video](https://play.library.utoronto.ca/watch/fe901685fb9f5e49b6fd8a0d7b7c45cc).

<img src='{{ '/assets/images/NVivo_Download_2.png' | relative_url }}' alt='The Nvivo homepage. Download nvivo for windows is highlighted. ' title='' width='1655' height='894' />

3\. Once NVivo 14 has been installed, start the application.

4\. Once you are prompted for the license key, copy and paste it from your email. Then accept the User Acknowledgement of License Terms and click Next.  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/NVivo14_installation_002.PNG' | relative_url }}' alt='Enter your NVivo 12 License Key and accept User Acknowledgement of License Terms highlighted' title='' width='598' height='648' />  
 

5\. When prompted, fill in your personal details for registration purposes and then click Activate.  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/NVivoAuthorize2.png' | relative_url }}' alt='License Activation popup with required fields and the Activate button highlighted' title='' width='600' height='578' />

That’s it! NVivo 14 should now be running on your computer.

Downloading, Installation, and Authorization (Mac)
==================================================

*Note: please be sure to download NVivo 14 specifically. The license key you received in your email will only work with NVivo 14\.*

1\. Log into [**myNVivo Portal**](https://portal.mynvivo.com/)using the account you just created. After logging in, under Downloads, click on “click here” to find NVivo 14\.  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/NVivo14_installation_001_2Mac.png' | relative_url }}' alt='MyNVivo Page with Download highlighted.' title='' width='1494' height='757' />  
2\. Install NVivo 14 for Mac following the usual procedure for installations on your computer.

3\. Once NVivo 14 has been installed, start the application.

a. If your Mac says that it cannot open NVivo 14, right click on the application and select Open, at the top of the menu.  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/Nvivo%20Installation_Mac_3a.png' | relative_url }}' alt='Right click on NVIVO and select Open to open the application' title='' width='694' height='490' />  
 

b. Then in the pop\-up window, click on Open.  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/Nvivo%20Installation_Mac_3b.png' | relative_url }}' alt='pop up stating macOS cannot verify NVivo developer' title='' width='355' height='561' />  
 

4\.  Please note you may be asked to allow NVivo Full Disk Access to your computer. To do this, select **Open Security \& Privacy** in the pop up, and select **NVivo** in your settings.

<img src='{{ '/assets/images/Nvivo%20Installation_Mac_4%281%29_1.png' | relative_url }}' alt='Choose Open Security and Privacy to allow NVivo to run' title='' width='353' height='434' /><img src='{{ '/assets/images/Nvivo%20Installation_Mac_4%282%29%20New.png' | relative_url }}' alt='Select NVivo under the Full Disk Access tab in Security and Privacy settings' title='' width='738' height='649' />

5\. Once open, you will be prompted to login to your myNVivo account.  You can either log in, or provide enterprise key to activate. If you choose to log in, you will then be given the option to start a 14\-day trial, or to provide the enterprise key.  Select **or provide enterprise key to activate.** You can then paste the license key from your email, check off **I agree to the user acknowledgement of license terms** and click **Next**.  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/Nvivo%20Installation_Mac_4%281%29.png' | relative_url }}' alt='Activate Nvivo popup ' title='' width='721' height='329' />  
 

<img src='{{ '/assets/images/Nvivo%20Installation_Mac_4%282%29_2.png' | relative_url }}' alt='NVivo pop-up prompting user to enter their license key' title='' width='649' height='502' />  
 

6\. If prompted, fill in your personal details for registration purposes and then click Activate.  
<img src='{{ '/assets/images/NVivo_mac4.png' | relative_url }}' alt='License Activation popup with several required fields' title='' width='600' height='480' />  
 

That’s it! NVivo 14 should now be running on your computer.
